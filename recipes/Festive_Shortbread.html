Festive Shortbread

Description:

Great for the holidays.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups butter, softened
  • 1 ¾ cups sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 4 ½ cups sifted all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Cream butter, gradually beat in sugar. Add and beat in flour a part at a time.

  3. Wrap dough in wax paper and chill.

  4. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Allow dough to warm slightly; knead for 1 minute. Roll out dough on lightly floured board to 1/4 inch thickness.

  5. Cut with cookie cutters. Decorate as desired.

  6. Bake in preheated oven 14-20 minutes.
